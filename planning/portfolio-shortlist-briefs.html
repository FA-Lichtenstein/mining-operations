Brief 1 · Truck-shovel vs K-Tec scraper DES T2 T4 idea-02 · composite 4.70 · haulage-des

Problem & persona

Naledi’s superintendent asks whether adding K-Tec scraper trains can reduce unit cost on a 12 Mt/a waste-stripping push without starving ore feed. Conventional truck-shovel cycles dominate the pit; contractor haulage is common in the Bushveld. She needs a defensible DES comparison — cycle times, queueing, match factor, tonnes/shift — not a vendor brochure.

MVP vs stretch

ScopeDeliverables
MVP Workbench at /demo/haulage-des: panel A scenario picker; panel B D3 haul cycle map; panel C ECharts queue/throughput; panel D collapsible cycle anatomy explainer. Dual-scenario DES (truck-shovel vs scraper-train) in Web Worker; KPI panel (throughput, queue wait, match factor Nh); compare two runs in-session; optional IndexedDB save and JSON export. Seed from gen-truck-cycle.
Stretch Third scenario (IPCC fixed-charge sidebar per T4); Gorai Ch 10.8 analytic queuing overlay (5 vs 22 trucks); animated queue chart (PR-06 pattern #6); contractor shift calendar toggle; export PDF summary for superintendent pack.

Brief 3 · Fleet maintenance MDP T5 idea-06 · composite 3.93 · fleet-capacity-mdp

Problem & persona

Two shovels and eighteen trucks must hit 42 kt/d through the wet season, but unplanned downtime and PM windows compete for the same maintenance bay. Naledi needs a policy: when to pull units for PM vs run-to-failure, given contractor-maintained vs owner-maintained split — without OEM confidential rates. SME availability/utilisation (A, U, E = A×U) and Fig 22.1 PM cost curve anchor the model.

MVP vs stretch

ScopeDeliverables
MVP Discrete MDP: states {operating, PM, down} × criticality class; policy iteration in Worker; heatmap of optimal action by state; 52-week simulation replay vs static PM calendar.
Stretch Multi-unit fleet (shovel + truck classes); link to haulage DES (#brief-02) when units down reduce throughput; P2041 capacity_series drift; export policy JSON for superintendent review.

Brief 4 · Open-pit short-term schedule optimizer T1 idea-01 · composite 3.86 · pit-schedule-gantt

Problem & persona

Quarterly pushback review: which blocks to mine in which periods to maximise NPV while respecting bench-down precedence, shovel capacity, and soft 4E grade targets? Naledi cannot run Whittle in-browser; she needs a toy instance (≤500 blocks) with honest Gorai Ch 5.4 / Handbook Ch 29.4 citation anchors and a Gantt the team can edit.

MVP vs stretch

ScopeDeliverables
MVP Bench map (GeoJSON) + period Gantt from pre-solved toy MIP; toggle soft vs hard metal penalty; block precedence validator; gen-block-model seed (~200 blocks).
Stretch Cloud Function OR-Tools re-solve on user-edited subset; NPV waterfall vs baseline; export schedule CSV; Kiruna 69.6% deviation narrative sidebar (Handbook Ch 31).
