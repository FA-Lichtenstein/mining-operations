Boucherie & van Dijk — Markov Decision Processes in Practice (2017)

Task ID: PR-12 · Date: 2026-05-27 · Upstream deps: PR-01 · Source: sources/processed/Richard J_ Boucherie, Nico M_ van Dijk - Markov Decision Processes in Practice (2017).txt · Themes: T1–T6 · Read depth: deep Ch 1–3, 8–9, 19; selective Ch 10, 13; TOC skim Ch 4–7, 11–12, 14–18, 20–21

This Springer handbook (Vol. 248, ISORMS; Hillier foreword) is a curated collection of 21 application chapters across six domains — general theory, healthcare, transportation, production, communications, and finance — each following problem → MDP formulation → solution → validation. The text contains no mining case studies; portfolio value is method transfer for capacity-constrained stochastic control under uncertainty (T5), haulage/fleet dispatch (T1/T2), and crew/equipment staffing. Deep read centred on Ch 3 (ADP via nomadic trucker, freight consolidation, hospital tactical planning), healthcare staffing/scheduling (Ch 8–9), and call-centre flexible staffing (Ch 19), with explicit mapping from the CV Honours nurse-workforce MDP (PR-03) to in-book healthcare chapters and mining reframes. Five seeds below; full scorecards for ranked top three (B, A, E); Seed C (~3.77) also clears the PR-19 threshold.

6. Portfolio seeds

Five seeds tagged T1–T6; chapter citations for each. Rank order by composite: B (4.24) → A (4.16) → E (3.78) → C (~3.77) → D (~3.25, below 3.5). Seed D kept for T1/T5 LP-ADP breadth; Seed E for Honours structural match (Ch 3.4) despite Effort L.

Seed A — Nomadic trucker ADP haul dispatch T1 T2

Source: Ch 3.2 (nomadic trucker MDP; post-decision state Sect. 3.2.3; basis-function VFA); Ch 3.3 (consolidation contrast).

Idea: Single haul truck on synthetic pit load board (256-cell grid or PR-07b locations): daily decision to reposition empty, accept loaded move, or wait; ADP with post-decision states when exact VI fails. Compare myopic “nearest load” vs learned policy — direct haulage narrative complementing Hillier queue DES (PR-10 Seed A).

Seed B — Flexible shift crew staffing MDP T5

Source: Ch 19.2–19.3 (permanent st + flexible ft; service level SL ≥ α; penalty P; known/unknown λ); Ch 2.3 (M/M/s relative values as sanity check).

Idea: Model a shift as m intervals with time-varying truck-arrival intensity; core crew fixed, contractor slots adjustable at epoch set T. Minimize staffing cost subject to soft dispatch SLA — explicit reframe of Honours nurse MDP and PR-03 T5 flagship path.

Seed C — Mobile unit dispatch (ambulance analog) T1 T2

Source: Ch 9 (MDP state: idle units + future incident location; heuristic from optimal policy; 13% late-arrival improvement vs closest-idle).

Idea: Two field-service water trucks or shovel-support units on a simplified pit map; stochastic breakdown locations. Policy heatmap vs closest-idle benchmark — quantified KPI uplift mirrors Ch 9 validation style.

Seed D — Workshop appointment LP-ADP T1 T5

Source: Ch 8.2–8.3 (state (u,v,w); patient types; wait targets; overtime; LP-ADP affine VFA).

Idea: Book maintenance bay slots for truck PM jobs with priority classes and turnaround targets; booking guidelines vs myopic fill. Effort L — LP-ADP layer likely needs Cloud Function; lower composite (~3.25).

Seed E — Multi-stage crew capacity network (hospital tactical) T5

Source: Ch 3.4 (queues J, resources R, ηr,t, waiting-time costs cj,u; transition qj,i).

Idea: Map pit stages (drill → load → haul) as queues; crew hours as resources; ADP or truncated MDP for admission/treatment analog. Strongest structural match to Honours nurse project; Effort L, composite ~3.78.
