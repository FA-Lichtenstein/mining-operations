Open Data & APIs (Mining)

Task ID: PR-07 · Date: 2026-05-27 · Upstream deps: PR-01, PR-02 · Themes: T1–T6 · Out of scope: CV matrix (PR-03), synthetic generators (PR-07b)

South Africa and broader Africa offer strong macro and spatial open data for mining portfolio demos — production time series, mine directories, geology, closure-risk atlases, and commodity benchmarks — but almost no public operational datasets (block models, dispatch logs, survey reconciliations) and no government bulk API for mining rights (SAMRAD legacy or Project Indwe). This doc catalogues 19 sources with licensing notes, API/machine-readability ratings, and per-theme (T1–T6) data-package recommendations. Synthetic mine-level seeds remain mandatory for T1/T2/T3 demos; see PR-07b.

Scope and method

  • Geography: Primary = South Africa (Stats SA, DMPR/DMRE, CGS, WRC, Minerals Council); secondary = SADC/Africa (CGS SADC series, USGS Africa geodatabase, Mozambique cadastre REST).
  • Boundary: Public or requestable datasets only; no proprietary mine feeds. CV alignment matrix is PR-03; parameter generators are PR-07b.
  • Research date: 2026-05-27. Note: DMRE split into DMPR (minerals/petroleum, effective April 2025) and Department of Electricity and Energy; legacy dmre.gov.za may redirect. Prefer dmpr.gov.za for operating-mines and licensing portals.
  • API column: REST/OGC = programmatic query; Bulk = ZIP/CSV/PDF download; Portal = interactive only; Subscription = email or paid gate.

Per-theme data package recommendations

Each package lists a public anchor (credibility), derived seed files (what to pre-process for Angular+Firebase), and synthetic gap (PR-07b).

T1 Short/medium-term scheduling & sequencing

Public anchor
DMPR Operating Mines (D1) filtered to one commodity hub (e.g. PGM Bushveld or Northern Cape manganese) + CGS 1:1M geology for domain boundaries.
Derived seeds
entities.csv — mine name, owner, commodity, mine_type, province (from provincial HTML scrape or D1 PDF); facilities.geojson — USGS Africa processing/plant points merged with D1 entities (manual geocode or province centroids).
Calibration
B1/ME Bulletin commodity production totals → annual tonnage caps per entity group.
Synthetic gap (PR-07b)
gen-block-model → blocks.csv, precedence_edges.csv, blocks.geojson — block precedence, shovel/extraction rates, geotech slope constraints.

T2 DES/ABS simulation (haulage, equipment, contractors)

Public anchor
Stats SA P2041 monthly production indices by mineral group + USGS Africa infrastructure (roads, ports, rail) for network graph.
Derived seeds
throughput_priors.json — mean/variance of monthly production by commodity; network.graphml — simplified haulage graph between USGS facility nodes and nearest port/rail.
Calibration
Minerals Council employment (~470k direct, 2025 pocketbook) → fleet/crew order-of-magnitude.
Synthetic gap (PR-07b)
gen-truck-cycle → cycles.csv, fleet_events.csv, fleet_state.json — inter-arrival times, load–haul–dump cycles, breakdown MTBF (PR-05 #01–#03).

T3 Production reconciliation & spatial KPIs

Public anchor
Stats SA P2041 production and sales series (monthly) — national reconciliation benchmark (production vs sales lag).
Derived seeds
benchmark_kpi.csv — month, commodity_group, production_index, sales_index, delta_pct; suitable for SPC control-chart demos at aggregate level.
Calibration
DMPR ME Bulletin quarterly tables for sector-level variance bands.
Synthetic gap (PR-07b)
gen-plan-survey-var → reconciliation_daily.csv, bench_variance.csv, survey_polygons.geojson (PR-05 #04–#05).

T4 Techno-economic trade-offs & transition timing

Public anchor
USGS MCS 2025 — PGM/gold/coal/iron ore price & reserve trajectories; CGS Bushveld/PGM map for geological context; Minerals Council Facts & Figures for SA cost/GDP context.
Derived seeds
commodity_macro.csv — 5-year USGS salient stats for 4–6 SA-relevant commodities; geology_context.json — metadata links to CGS map layers.
Calibration
Project Indwe public map — illustrate tenure/constraint overlays (qualitative only until bulk export exists).
Synthetic gap (PR-07b)
Re-run gen-truck-cycle with scenario_id=ipcc-conveyor + gen-block-model tonnage; WSM uses commodity_macro.csv (PR-05 #02, #08, #15).

T5 Forecasting / MDP / capacity-constrained planning

Public anchor
Stats SA P2041 long monthly series (1980+) + QES via Stats SA/DataFirst for sector employment trends.
Derived seeds
capacity_series.csv — rolling 12-month production, YoY%, seasonal indices; workforce_prior.json — mining employment share from Minerals Council + QES.
Calibration
USGS world production share for SA commodities → export-demand scenario bounds.
Synthetic gap (PR-07b)
gen-truck-cycle → fleet_state.json shift snapshots calibrated to capacity_series.csv.

T6 Closure liability & risk-based financial modelling

Public anchor
WRC Mine Closure Risk & Opportunity Atlas (closure likelihood, community deprivation) + Global Tailings Portal (SA TSF inventory) + WRC Mine Water Atlas threat rasters.
Derived seeds
closure_risk.csv — mine name, likelihood_score, water_threat_class, tailings_flag; tailings_sa.geojson — filtered Global Tailings SA points (after GRID-Arendal request or manual portal export).
Calibration
Esau et al. / MDPI closure rating methodology for indicator weights; D1 mine type for underground vs surface water threat lookup.
Synthetic gap (PR-07b)
No generator for liability cashflows — join closure_risk.csv + block site metadata; MC draws in demo Worker (PR-05 #10–#11).

Gaps and risks

  • No mine-level operations data: Public sources stop at national/commodity or mine-directory granularity. T1/T2/T3 portfolio modules cannot claim “live pit data” without PR-07b synthetics.
  • No open cadastre API: Neither SAMRAD nor Project Indwe publishes bulk mining-rights GeoJSON/CSV; ICE DMRE API is commercial only. Portfolio maps must use D1 entity points + qualitative tenure screenshots, not live rights sync.
  • D1 extraction: Provincial HTML tables ease scraping vs PDF-only, but there is still no official CSV — budget time for structured export or province-centroid geocoding.
  • DMPR transition: DMRE → DMPR split (April 2025) and Project Indwe rollout may shift URLs; SAMRAD retirement is provincial and ongoing.
  • CGS commercial boundary: Geology WMS is non-commercial; portfolio hosted on a public URL should use downloadable shapefiles + attribution, not embed paid layers.
  • Tailings bulk access: Global Tailings CSV requires email request — plan lead time before T6 demo.
  • Overlap with PR-09: SA mining context doc should cite this catalog rather than re-research sources.
